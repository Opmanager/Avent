Consultation & Coaching

Initial Consultation Meeting

We are only limited by the things that we allow to limit us. The keys to success and productivity are already in our pocket. You just need someone to show you how to unlock the door. We work with our clients to identify their unique needs and offer recommendations for the next steps. It is essential to schedule an initial meeting to provide us with the opportunity to listen to your needs, identify problem areas, and design a plan tailored to your organization. The goals of the initial meeting are to:
1) To obtain an objective assessment of your organization’s culture and how it’s helping or hindering the achievement of the mission.
2) To collect data that will help us identify workplace factors that may be adversely impacting individual and organizational effectiveness.
3) To assess education, training and facilitation needs.

The Coaching
